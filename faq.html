FAQ

What time should I arrive?

The Event starts at 4:00 PM and will be at Ukraine House in Kalorama, Washington DC. The address is 2134 Kalorama Rd NW, Washington, DC 20008. We recommend arriving on time. Unless it rains very hard, the Event will be held Outside!

What’s the parking situation?

Street parking is available but can be competitive. The Woodley Park-Zoo/Adams Morgan metro station is a ~10 minute walk from Ukraine House.

Can I bring a plus one?

Your partner is invited :)

Are kids welcome?

Yes for the first part of the day! The Event and Dinner will have a few kids. After dinner, we’ll walk together to Roofers Union for The Party, which will be ~for the adults~

What about dietary restrictions?

It’ll be vegetarian and vegan friendly. It will be meat unfriendly. Please note any allergies or restrictions on your RSVP!

Is there a hotel block?

Yes! We have room blocks at the Washington Hilton and the Churchill Hotel, both about 5 mins walk from Ukraine House. Here is the link for the Washington Hilton ($159 per night + fees). We will add the link for the Churchill Hotel ($139 per night + fees) once we have it. The event is called “Gaby and Andrew’s party” if you need to search it. Of course, feel free to stay elsewhere if you want. Europeans, please contact us before booking anything!

What should I wear?

Chic, August-friendly, comfortable. Think somewhere between "nice dinner" and "daytime wedding." When in doubt, think "what would Tom Zanger think looks cool?"

Is there an open bar?

Duh

What about gifts?

No! We'll put together a pot closer to the day which will go to charity donations if you want, but the expectation is that you give nothing.

What's the afterparty vibe?

Something like having a gummy and getting confused which uber is for you.

I have another question.

Just email us! draganovandrew@gmail.com or gabygalvin@gmail.com (or both!)
